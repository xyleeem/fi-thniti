About Us

FI THNITI is a team of young Tunisian entrepreneurs with one simple goal :
to make life easier. We connect people who need to send items with travelers heading the same way,
offering a more affordable, faster, and secure alternative to traditional delivery services.
Our mission is to simplify the delivery process and create a trusted platform for everyone.
